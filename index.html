The Legend of Zelda’s Cooking Pot

Inspiration

I love the Legend of Zelda Games. So, when we were tasked to 3D render a fruit bowl in Bleder, with any sort of creative aspect we desired, I decided to give it a twist by making the famous cooking pot in Zelda.

Given that you can cook fruit in it as well, it still be considered a fruit bowl.... right?

The Ingredients:

After settling on a concept, it was time to decide what elements I needed to 3D model besides the bowl and the wood.  My choice was based on what items are classic and commonly found in the Zelda world.

Even though the Hylian Mushroom is not a fruit, it is an easily recognisable object that makes the concept identifiable.

The 3D models

All of the 3D models made in this project were made in Blender by me. Given that it was not required to add textures or shading, I left the shapes as they were In order to make the design more recognisable, I made the elements fall into the bowl just as they do in the game.
